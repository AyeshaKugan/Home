Knitting Stitches

Here are some basic stitches you can do after mastering knitting and purling.

  1. Garter Stitch
  2. Stockinette Stitch
  3. Rib Stitch
  4. Seed Stitch
  5. Basketweave Stitch

Garter Stitch

Knit all stitches

Stockinette Stitch

Row 1: Knit all stitches

Row 2: Purl all stitches

Rib Stitch

Row 1: *Knit 1, Purl 1*

Row 2: Knit the stitches you purled, purl the stitches you knit.

Seed Stitch

Cast on an odd number of stitches

All Rows: *Knit 1, Purl 1* Knit 1

Basketweave Stitch

Cast on multiples of 4

Row 1-4: *Knit 4, Purl 4*

Row 5-8: *Purl 4, Knit 4*
